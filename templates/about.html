Hello! This is a personal project for my HCDE310: Interactive Systems Design and Technology course showing various bike incidents utilizing the Bikewise API around a given location using the Google Maps API.

Feel free to alert me of any troubles or issues. I will sort out the few interface problems eventually, but I hope that the data and information is valuable and presented in an understandable manner.

This application was created to allow bike riders to scout the area they are in and determine if it is "safe." My primary motivation began with allowing bike riders to understand if the vicinity was safe to lock up their bike for an extended period of time by seeing if there are any nearby thefts. As I expanded this project, I added the ability to search other incidents such as hazards and crashes to allow bike riders to be more alert about their routes and any potential troubles or danger.

"Bikespotters" are encouraged to cross reference this web app and assist owners in their search for stolen bikes. There are many success stories on Bike Index and the community is very supportive, so this web app may serve as a tool for "bikespotters" to see if any bikes they see have been reported.

Have a report?

Report any problems at Bike Index.

I am aware about a few troubles, however they are due to the nature of the Bikewise API. Attempts that result in older information tend to lack descriptions and I am thus unable to show any relevant information. I have added text to these markers on the map to indicate the issue. One possible solution is to filter all results based on their age and sort from there, however, because I am unable to know if these incidents are resolved, I have chosen to keep them within this web app for complete clarity and visibility as a precaution.
